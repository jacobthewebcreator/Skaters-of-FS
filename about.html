About Skaters of FS

Celebrating the art, athleticism, and soul of figure skating from around the globe. skaters of fs

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant, visual showcase that honors the dedication and passion of figure skaters in both the men's and women's disciplines. We aim to inspire fans, new and old, by curating a collection of moments that capture the breathtaking beauty and raw power of the sport. We believe that every spin, jump, and sequence tells a story, and our goal is to provide a platform where these stories can shine.

Our Story

Skaters of FS was born from a lifelong love for figure skating. Frustrated by the fragmented nature of finding high-quality content, our founder envisioned a single, beautifully designed space where the greatest moments on ice could be appreciated. What started as a small passion project has grown into a community hub for enthusiasts who share an appreciation for the skaters who push the boundaries of what's possible on a blade's edge.

From late-night competition streams to early-morning practices, we've lived the skating life and want to share that passion with the world. This site is a tribute to the athletes who inspire us daily.

The Team

We are a small but dedicated team of designers, developers, and figure skating aficionados. We spend our days curating content, managing our social media presence, and brainstorming new ways to celebrate the sport we love. Thank you for being a part of our journey.
